About

Nestled in the vibrant culture of Kerala, Thrissur, Fair & Glow Beauty Clinic offers a refreshing beauty experience in a warm, friendly setting. Our professional team of passionate Beauty Artists is committed to providing top-notch services.
Established in 2014 by an internationally certified beautician, we prioritize using high-quality products that match our clients' needs and budgets. Quality is our unwavering standard. Over the years, our loyal clients have been our inspiration to continually innovate our services.
At Fair & Glow, we blend education, beauty, people, and fashion to deliver highly personalized services in a welcoming environment. We take pride in offering a wide range of treatments that ensure you leave feeling your best. Discover the world of beauty and relaxation at Fair & Glow Beauty Clinic. Your journey to beauty begins here.
